City of Medina Dates

Medina is famous for its wide variety of dates, which are enjoyed for their natural sweetness, cultural value, and health benefits. Dates are often served with Arabic coffee as a sign of hospitality.
They are rich in fiber, minerals, and vitamins, making them both delicious and nutritious. There are many different types, from soft and sweet to dry and chewy, each with its unique taste.
Medina dates are also exported worldwide, sharing the culture of the Kingdom with the rest of the world.

Popular Types of Medina Dates

  • Ajwa
  • Kholas
  • Sukkary
  • Medjool
